About Us

Our chess club is one of the best in New York. Many famous American chess players who became world champions attended classes at our club. And now we continue our glorious traditions and offer everyone to learn how to play chess, the oldest and most interesting intellectual game of the planet. We conduct classes for children and adults, prepare for the championships, and give something more than just a nice hobby.

Experience

Our chess club has existed for more than 50 years, a huge number of chess players have studied here.

Best Tutors

Our tutors are current or former professional players who have gained international recognition.

Practice

In our club, you can practice with the best chess players in America and take part in tournaments.

What We Offer

We hold chess classes for children and adults.

Classes for Adults

By enrolling in our club, you can quickly learn all the intricacies of the game. Our tutors work with both beginners and experienced players. We will provide you with quality theory and practice, explain techniques that will help you analyze the situation on the board faster.

Classes for Children

Chess helps a child increase his intellectual skills and mathematical abilities. Our tutors have vast experience in teaching children. We can clearly explain to any child how this game works and instill in him the principles of chess thinking and line of thinking.

Championship Preparation

Our chess club will help professionals and amateurs prepare to participate in tournaments. We will help to develop tactics, work out plans for the game, etc. We conduct complete and thorough training of chess players willing to take part in any chess championships.

Events

Our chess club is open to everyone! We often hold free events for those who want to get acquainted with the fascinating world of chess.

12 AUG, 12:30 PM Paul Foster

Lecture on Game Strategy

This lecture will be useful for both beginners and experienced players. You will learn how to choose a strategy when playing against a particular opponent, what are the strategies, what tactics are chosen in this or that case.

18 AUG, 5:00 PM Ann King

Introduction to the Game of Chess

Do you want to learn how to play chess? Then be sure to visit our open master class for beginner chess players. Our tutors will give you the basics of the game of chess. You also can meet our teachers and other members of our club.

28 AUG, 2:30 PM Veselin Topalov

Master-class by Veselin Topalov

Save the date to see the game of one of the best chess players of our time. Veselin Topalov will share his experience, show several of his signature moves and techniques, thanks to which he could become a two-time world champion.

28 AUG, 05:00 PM Veselin Topalov

Autograph Session With Veselin Topalov

After the master class, you can have a talk with the world chess champion! Veselin Topalov will hold an autograph session, and you will get the opportunity to take a picture with one of the most famous chess players in history.
